Yanjiang Guo

I am a third year PhD student of computer science at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University, advised by Jianyu Chen. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsighua University.

My research focuses on Embodied AI, a field that intersects robotics and generative AI. Specifically, I employ advanced generative models, such as large language models and visual diffusion models, to equip agents with prior physical knowledge. This enhancement bolsters their decision-making and control capabilities in open environments.
